Receipt Scanner for Accountants UK — The 2026 Guide

Stop retyping receipts. Here's how AI-powered receipt scanning saves UK accountants hours on expense processing — and which tools actually work.

Receipts are the unglamorous side of accounting. Every client has a shoebox full of them. Every bookkeeper spends hours typing them up. And unlike bank statements — which at least have consistent formats — receipts come in every shape, size, language, and currency imaginable.

AI-powered receipt scanning has changed this. You can now take a photo of a crumpled till receipt and get a structured Excel spreadsheet with line items, totals, VAT, and merchant details — in seconds. Here's what UK accountants need to know.

How AI Receipt Scanning Works

  1. Capture: Take a photo of the receipt with your phone, or upload a scanned image
  2. AI extraction: The AI reads the receipt — merchant name, date, individual line items, amounts, VAT, totals
  3. Structured output: You get a clean Excel spreadsheet with colour-coded columns, ready for import into your accounting software

The key advance in 2026 is that modern AI doesn't just OCR the text — it understands the receipt structure. It knows which numbers are line items vs totals, which text is the merchant name vs a random footer, and how to handle multi-currency receipts.
