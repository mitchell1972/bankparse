Detailed Breakdown: What You Actually Get

1. BankScan AI — $9.99/mo (Starter)

Built specifically for UK and US accountants. Handles the messy multi-line formats that generic converters choke on — especially HSBC, NatWest, and older Barclays statements. Also processes store receipts (photo → line items in Excel).

  • Unique strength: Deep UK bank format knowledge — 16+ UK banks supported, including challenger banks (Monzo, Starling, Revolut, Tide) that most US tools ignore
  • Weakness: Newer product — smaller user base than DocuClipper
  • Best for: UK accountants and bookkeepers processing 5–50 client statements/month

2. Statement Desk — $19/mo (Professional)

Clean UI with Google Sheets and Xero integration. Charges by transaction rows rather than statements, which can work out cheaper or more expensive depending on statement length.

  • Unique strength: Google Sheets integration, Xero export
  • Weakness: Row-based pricing can get expensive with long statements; no receipt processing
  • Best for: Users who need direct Xero/Google Sheets integration

3. DocuClipper — $39/mo (Starter)

The market leader by user base. Strong on features — financial analysis, fraud detection (50+ indicators), AWS Marketplace listing. Charges by pages processed, not statements.

  • Unique strength: Fraud detection, financial analysis, SOC 2 Type II certified
  • Weakness: 4× more expensive than BankScan AI at entry level; page-based pricing means long statements cost more
  • Best for: Large firms needing enterprise features and compliance certifications

4. Lido — $29/mo

Positioned as a premium option targeting CPA firms. US-focused with limited UK bank format support.

  • Unique strength: CPA-specific workflow features
  • Weakness: Limited UK bank support, no receipt processing, higher price
  • Best for: US CPA firms that don't need UK bank formats

5. CapyParse — ~$25/mo

General-purpose document parser that adapts to any bank format via AI. Less accounting-specific than other options.

  • Unique strength: Works with any bank format globally
  • Weakness: Not accounting-specific — no receipt processing, no bookkeeping integrations
  • Best for: Users needing a flexible, bank-agnostic parser

6. RocketStatements — $29/mo

US-focused with QuickBooks Desktop EOL migration tools. Targeting property management as a vertical.

  • Unique strength: QuickBooks Desktop migration playbook
  • Weakness: US-only focus, higher price, no UK bank support
  • Best for: US property managers migrating from QuickBooks Desktop
