2. Statement Desk — Best for Xero Integration

Price: $19/mo (Professional) | Free trial: 3 free conversions

Powered by Claude AI with a clean, modern interface. The standout feature is native Xero and Google Sheets integration — if those are your primary tools, Statement Desk saves you an export-import step on every statement.

Standout features: One-click Xero export, Google Sheets integration, AI transaction categorization, merchant name normalization, bulk processing (up to 20 files).

Limitations: Charges by transaction rows, not statements — long statements eat through your allowance. No receipt processing. Nearly 2× the price of BankScan AI.

Best for: Xero users who want the convenience of one-click export and don't need receipt processing.

3. DocuClipper — Best for Enterprise & Compliance

Price: $39/mo (Starter) | Free trial: 14-day

The market leader by user base — and for good reason. SOC 2 Type II certified, fraud detection with 50+ indicators, built-in financial analysis, and listed on AWS Marketplace. If your firm needs enterprise-grade compliance, this is the one.

Standout features: Fraud detection, financial ratio analysis, SOC 2 certification, API access on all plans, unlimited users per subscription, international bank support.

Limitations: 4× the price of BankScan AI at entry level. Charges by pages, not statements. Overkill for solo bookkeepers who just want quick PDF-to-Excel conversion.

Best for: Large accounting firms, regulated industries, and teams that need compliance certifications and fraud detection.

4. Lido — US CPA Specialist

Price: $29/mo | Free trial: Limited

Built for US CPA firms with workflow features tailored to American accounting practices. Limited UK bank support makes it less relevant for British accountants.

Best for: US CPA firms that don't need UK bank format support.

5. CapyParse — Best for International Formats

Price: ~$25/mo | Free tier available

General-purpose document parser that uses AI to adapt to any bank format. Less accounting-specific than dedicated converters but flexible enough to handle obscure international banks.

Best for: Users dealing with non-standard or international bank formats who need maximum flexibility.

6. RocketStatements — US Property Management Focus

Price: $29/mo | Free trial: Limited

US-focused with a QuickBooks Desktop EOL migration playbook. Targeting property management as a vertical. No meaningful UK bank support.

Best for: US property managers and businesses migrating from QuickBooks Desktop.
