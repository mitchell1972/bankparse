A Note on the Pricing Models

Statement Desk charges by transaction rows (1,000 rows/mo on Professional). A single bank statement with 150 transactions uses 15% of your monthly allowance — before you even account for multi-line entries that can count as multiple rows.

BankScan AI charges by statement (120/mo on Starter). A 5-page HSBC statement with 300 transactions counts as 1 statement. For accountants processing client statements, this model is more predictable and often more generous.
